    Keep up to date with Activity Stream

    By Matt K. Cupertino, CA Level 4 (2,380 points) Version 19 53085 Views Last Modified: Oct 17, 2013 11:11 AM

    Activity Stream displays the latest replies to the discussions and topics that interest you. Customize your stream, reply to discussions, change your email preferences, and view unanswered questions—all from a single page.

     

     

    To view your Activity Stream, sign in with your Apple ID and click Activity from any Apple Support Communities page.

     

     

     

     

    Keep up to date

    Activity Stream displays the most recent replies for every discussion you’ve started or replied to. Scroll through the page to see a summary of all activity since your last visit. If you want to read an entire post, click Show More at the top of the post you want to read.

     

    The entire post will display on the page, so you don't have to leave your Activity Stream.

     

    Continue scrolling to view the rest of your updates. When you’ve seen everything new since your last visit, you’ll see a notice that reads, “End of New Updates”.

     

     

     

     

    Customize your Activity Stream

    You can customize your Activity Stream by following additional topics or removing discussions you’re no longer interested in.

     

    To add or remove communities:

     

    1. Visit the community you want to follow. For example, “iPhone in Business and Education.”
    2. To add the community from your Activity Stream, click Follow. Your Activity Stream will now display updates for all new posts in that community.
    3. To remove the community from your Activity Stream, click Following. The icon will turn grey to indicate that you're no longer following the community.

     

    To remove a discussion:

     

    1. Locate the discussion in your Activity Stream.
    2. Click “Stop following this discussion”. Your activity stream will no longer display updates about that discussion.

     

     

    Change your email preferences

    When you add discussions or communities to your Activity Stream, you will also receive notifications via email. You can choose to stop or start receiving email notifications at any time.

     

    To turn email updates off or on:

     

    1. Go to your Activity Stream by clicking Activity on any Apple Support Communities page.
    2. Near the upper-right corner of the Activity Stream page, click the Email Updates switch. You will no longer receive updates via email.
    3. If you have turned off email updates and would like to start receiving them again, click the switch to turn them on.

     

     

    Find questions to answer

    The Unanswered Questions stream displays questions that haven’t been solved. You can customize the Unanswered Questions stream to only display questions from your favorite communities. When you find a question to answer, you can quickly reply without leaving the page.

     

    To add communities to your Unanswered Questions stream:

     

    1. Go to your Activity Stream by clicking Activity from any Apple Support Communities page.
    2. Click “Unanswered Questions” to view the Stream.
    3. Click the gear icon on the right side of the Unanswered Questions button.
    4. A list of communities will appear. Scroll through the list or use the Search field to find the community you want to follow.
    5. Once you find the right community, drag it to the column on the left, or click the Add button.
    6. Click "Done" on the Unanswered Questions button.

     

     

     

    To remove communities from your Unanswered Questions Stream:

     

    1. Go to your Activity Stream by clicking Activity from any Apple Support Communities page.
    2. Click “Unanswered Questions” to view the Stream.

     

    • Click the Gear icon to customize the stream.
    • In the column on the left, find the community you want to remove. Click the X next to the community name.
