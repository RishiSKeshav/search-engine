    Award points, level up, and earn new privileges

    By bryan_w. Community Hosts (15 points) Version 12 6356 Views Last Modified: Oct 31, 2014 12:23 PM

    Apple Support Communities includes a reputation system that lets you say thanks to helpful members and earn points when you post quality responses. As you collect points, you earn status and additional community privileges.

     

     

     

    Mark helpful posts

    When you post a question and others respond, you can mark the most helpful posts so they are easier for other members to find. Plus, you encourage helpful members, who receive reputation points when you mark their answers.

     

    Mark helpful posts in one of two ways:

     

    • Click This solved my question to choose one response that best answers your question. A copy of the answer will appear below your question.

     

    • Click This helped me to mark up to two responses that offered useful information or workarounds. A link to each response will appear below your question.

    To mark a post from the community:

     

    1. Sign in to Apple Support Communities with your Apple ID.
    2. Go to the response you want to mark.
    3. Click one of the reputation buttons below the response.

     

     

     

     

    To mark a post from an email notification, click one of the reputation buttons at the bottom of the message.

     

     

     

    Earn points, status levels, and privileges

    When another community member marks your response, you receive reputation points. Helpful responses are worth five points, and responses that answer the member’s question are worth ten.

     

    You can see a user's status level and points next to each post.

     

     

     

    As you collect reputation points, your status level increases and you receive additional privileges.
