    Browse helpful communities content

    By Matt K. Cupertino, CA Level 4 (2,380 points) Version 14 10160 Views Last Modified: Oct 17, 2013 11:50 AM

    When you want to browse Discussions or User Tips, try the Content page. You can sort and view content by type, date, topic, and more. Or, you can use the People page to see all the content by your favorite community members.

     

     

     

     

    The Content page

    To access the Content page, click Content near the upper-right corner of any communities page.

     

     

    When you visit the content page, you’ll see a list of recent content from all communties. For each item in the list, you can see the number of Likes, Bookmarks, Comments, and Views the content has received. You can also see the date and time of the most recent response.

     

     

     

     

    To filter by content type, click one of the options on the bar above the content list. Choose from All Content, Announcements, User Tips, or Discussions.

     

     

     

     

    A popup menu allows you sort the content list. Choose from Title, Date of latest activity, or Date created.

     

    To filter the list by text, use the search field in the left sidebar.

     

     

     

     

    To filter by status, click one of the options in the left sidebar.

     

     

     

    • Drafts includes any content you have created, but have not yet published.
    • Authored displays all of the questions you have asked, discussions you have started, and User Tips you have created.
    • Participated contains any replies you have posted to another user’s content.
    • Following displays all discussions you are currently following.
    • Recently viewed shows all content you’ve viewed in the last few weeks.
    • All displays a list of all recent content.

     

     

     

    The People page

    Use the people page to find content written by your favorite members. To access the People page, click People near the upper-right corner of any communities page.

     

     

    On the people page, you can find specific members by typing a name into the search field labelled “Type to filter by text.”

     

     

    After a few moments, you will see a list of users with similar names. Click a username to view the member's Profile, recently authored content, and bookmarks.
