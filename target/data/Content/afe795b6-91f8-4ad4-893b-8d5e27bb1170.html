      Welcome to Apple Support Communities

      You can use Apple Support Communities to get help with Apple products from other users around the world. Browse existing answers, ask a question of your own, and share your expertise with others.

      Find answers and ask new questions

      Have a question? Use the Search or Ask a Question bar to quickly find an answer. If your question hasn’t been answered before, click “Submit my question to the community” to post a new question. Learn More

      Keep up to date with Activity Stream

      Activity Stream displays the latest replies to the discussions and topics that interest you. Customize your stream, reply to discussions, change your email preferences, and view unanswered questions—all from a single page. Learn More

      Award points, level up, and earn new privileges

      Apple Support Communities includes a reputation system that lets you say thanks to helpful members and earn points when you post quality responses. As you collect points, you earn status and additional community privileges. Learn More

      Browse helpful communities content

      When you want to browse Discussions or User Tips, try the Content page. You can sort and view content by type, date, topic, and more. Or, you can use the People page to see all the content by your favorite community members. Learn More

      Use advanced search features

      If you can’t find what you’re looking for with the Search or Ask a Question bar, try an advanced search. Click Search near the upper-right corner of any communities page. Type your search terms and press Return. Then use the advanced search tools to find what you need.
